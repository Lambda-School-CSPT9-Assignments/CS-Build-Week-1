Conway's Game of Life

The rules of Conway's Game of life are as follows:

  1. Any live cell with two or three live neighbours survives.
  2. Any dead cell with three live neighbours becomes a live cell.
  3. All other live cells die in the next generation. Similarly, all other dead cells stay dead.

The grid is preset with a random pattern, hit play to see it in action.

You can click on a cell while the simulation is puased to activate/deactivate it.

While the simulation is paused, you can click Next to manually step into the next interation.

Clicking on any of the presets below will load that preset.
